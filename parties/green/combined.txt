Affordable
Housing

LEARN MORE
Climate
Action

We will protect our kids and communities by cutting fossil fuel pollution and making Canada a world leader in clean energy.

LEARN MORE
Fair
Taxation

We will close unfair tax loopholes and take action to ensure the ultra-rich and big corporations pay their fair share.

LEARN MORE
Peace
& Security

We will protect Canadians from today's threats, strengthen our self-reliance, and continue to work for global peace.

LEARN MORE
More
Health Care

We will strengthen universal health care and expand coverage to include medicines, mental health, and more dental care.

LEARN MORE
Green
Economy

We will invest deeply in Canadian innovation, create good jobs in clean industries, and make sure no worker is left behind.

LEARN MORE
Strong
Democracy

We will rebuild trust in government, and strengthen democracy by updating our voting system so every vote counts.

LEARN MORE
Caring
for Canadians

We will create better support for Canadians at every stage of life, because we know caring for each other makes us stronger.

LEARN MORE
Stop The
Hate

We will fight all forms of hate and discrimination to make Canada a safer and more welcoming place for everyone.

LEARN MORE
Emergency
Preparedness

We will prepare for climate disasters, pandemics, and other crises before they happen, not just react after they strike.

LEARN MORE
Disability
Justice

We will ensure financial security for people with disabilities, and break down barriers to participation in all parts of life.

LEARN MORE
Indigenous
Reconciliation

We will respect Indigenous rights, support Indigenous sovereignty, and act on the Calls to Action and the Calls for Justice.
